Info

It all started in way back in 1822 when Mystery Babushka made her first batch of baklava. I ate the whole batch in just fifteen minutes. Man, it was tasty. Unfortunately, Mystery Babushka never told me her secret in the art of baklava-making. After the incident, I never saw her again. Now, years later, I be searching through my musty dusty basement only to find the recipes and started baking them in my stone oven. I started selling sandwiches and drinks and things were going fine. Then I got cat. Cat is good. Cat is great. Cat is amazing. We see shootup in popularity. Get more cat. Make cat dessert. Now we cat cafe. - Founder Unknown

The founders of our cafe have remained unknown for forever. We only know that they make very good food. The founders were never cat people but the cats came into the cafe so they had to let the cats stay. Now we are proud of our cats in our cat cafe.

Here at Cat Cafe, we have a variety of light dishes and beverages. We offer pastries and a variety of cat-themed specials.
